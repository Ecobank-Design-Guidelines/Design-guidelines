Grid



A grids systems main purpose is to help designers create consistent page layouts through predefined columns. It offers easy component placement, consistency over our product offering and helps designers get started quicker.

Overview

  • 0.1 Devices
  • 0.2 Reference table
  • 0.3 Placement
  • 0.4 Full width blocks

Devices

Home

The grid consists of 12 flexible columns with a max-width of 1260px and a fixed 30px gutter on screen sizes larger than 1024px. Columns are always centered within the main content area.

Note: The content area does not include the sidebar if a side element is used the content space is the remainder of the space.

News

On mobile, screen sizes equal or smaller than 420px the grid will be made up of 4 columns full-with and a fixed 10px padding either sides of a column that makes up a 20px gutter. Also the container has 10px padding on either side.

Contact

On mobile, screen sizes equal or smaller than 420px the grid will be made up of 4 columns full-with and a fixed 10px padding either sides of a column that makes up a 20px gutter. Also the container has 10px padding on either side.

About

On screen sizes, equal or smaller than 768px the grid will be made up of 8 columns full-with and a fixed 10px padding either sides of a column that makes up a 20px gutter. Also the container has 10px padding on either side.

Reference table

Placement

When placing components and elements, make sure that the left and right sides match up to the column edges and not succeeding into the gutter.

Full width blocks

When you require a full width element placement of the element will exceed into the column gutter on both sides, just keep in mind than the gutter of an element is not the full gutter in between column, only half of that space belongs to a column.
