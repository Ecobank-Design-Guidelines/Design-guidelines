Introduction

Welcome Message

The Ecobank Design System is a comprehensive resource hub, that acts as a single source of truth, governed by principles, guidelines and patterns and contains ready-made assets and components, that would help build exceptional experiences on our digital platforms.

Quality Products: Good design is not only a requirement but a deep responsibility to the customers we serve.

Design: A new experience awaits you and you get to bring it to life.

Developer: our design system helps us work together to build a great experience for all of partners.



About us

Our personality establishes the foundation of the Ecobank brand. It is a product of our mission, and a reflection of our culture, values, and promise to customers. We make sure that these traits come through in all of our customer-facing communications and content.
